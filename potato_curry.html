Recipe Revel




Ingredients

  • potatoes: 4 medium, peeled and cubed
  • Oil: 2 tablespoons
  • Mustard seeds: 1 teaspoon
  • Cumin seeds: 1 teaspoon
  • Onions: 2 medium, finely chopped
  • Tomatoes: 2 large, finely chopped or pureed
  • Garlic: 4 cloves, minced
  • Ginger: 1-inch piece, minced
  • Green chilies: 1-2, slit (optional)
  • Turmeric powder: 1/2 teaspoon
  • Red chili powder: 1 teaspoon
  • Coriander powder: 1 teaspoon
  • Cumin powder: 1 teaspoon
  • Garam masala: 1/2 teaspoon
  • Salt: to taste
  • Water: 1 1/2 cups
  • Fresh cilantro: for garnish

Process

  • step1: Heat the oil in a large pan or pot over medium heat.
  • step2: Add the mustard seeds and cumin seeds. Let them splutter for a few seconds.
  • step3: Add the finely chopped onions and sauté until they turn golden brown.
  • step4: Add the minced garlic, ginger, and slit green chilies. Sauté for another 2-3 minutes until fragrant.
  • step5: Add the turmeric powder, red chili powder, coriander powder, and cumin powder. Sauté for a minute to let the spices bloom.
  • step6: Add the chopped tomatoes (or tomato puree) and cook until the tomatoes break down and the oil starts to separate from the mixture, about 10 minutes.
  • step7: Add the cubed potatoes and stir to coat them well with the spice mixture.
  • step8: Pour in 1 1/2 cups of water and bring to a boil.
  • step9: Reduce the heat to a simmer, cover the pan, and cook until the potatoes are tender, about 15-20 minutes.
  • step10: Stir occasionally and add more water if needed to prevent sticking and to achieve your desired consistency.
  • step11: Once the potatoes are cooked, add salt to taste and stir in the garam masala.
  • step12: Let the curry simmer for another 2-3 minutes to blend the flavors.
