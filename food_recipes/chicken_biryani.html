Recipe Revel




Ingredients

  • Chicken: 500 grams, cut into medium pieces
  • Yogurt: 1/2 cup
  • Turmeric powder: 1/2 teaspoon
  • Red chili powder: 1 teaspoon
  • Coriander powder: 1 teaspoon
  • Cumin powder: 1/2 teaspoon
  • Garam masala: 1 teaspoon
  • Salt: 1 teaspoon
  • Lemon juice: 1 tablespoon
  • Ginger-garlic paste: 2 tablespoons
  • Basmati rice: 2 cups
  • Water: 4 cups
  • Bay leaf: 1
  • Cinnamon stick: 1-inch piece
  • Cloves: 4
  • Cardamom pods: 3
  • Salt: 1 teaspoon
  • Onions: 3 large, thinly sliced
  • Tomatoes: 2 medium, chopped
  • Green chilies: 2, slit
  • Ginger-garlic paste: 1 tablespoon
  • Fresh cilantro: 1/4 cup, chopped
  • Mint leaves: 1/4 cup, chopped
  • Oil: 3 tablespoons
  • Ghee: 2 tablespoons
  • Saffron: a pinch, soaked in 2 tablespoons of warm milk (optional)
  • Milk: 1/4 cup (optional)
  • Biryani masala: 1 tablespoon (optional)
  • Salt: to taste

Process

  • step1:In a large bowl, combine the yogurt, turmeric powder, red chili powder, coriander powder, cumin powder, garam masala, salt, lemon juice, and ginger-garlic paste.
  • step2:Add the chicken pieces to the marinade and mix well to coat all the pieces. Cover and let it marinate in the refrigerator for at least 1 hour, preferably overnight.
  • step3: Rinse the basmati rice in water until the water runs clear. Soak the rice in water for 30 minutes.
  • step4:In a large pot, bring 4 cups of water to a boil. Add the bay leaf, cinnamon stick, cloves, cardamom pods, and salt.
  • step5:Add the soaked rice and cook until the rice is 70-80% cooked (the rice should still have a bite to it). Drain the rice and set aside.
  • step6:Heat oil in a large pan or pot over medium heat. Add the sliced onions and fry until they turn golden brown. Remove half of the fried onions and set aside for layering and garnish.
  • step7: Add the ginger-garlic paste and slit green chilies to the remaining onions in the pan. Sauté for 2-3 minutes until the raw smell disappears.
  • step8:Add the chopped tomatoes and cook until they become soft and the oil starts to separate from the mixture.
  • step9:Add the marinated chicken pieces along with the marinade. Cook for 10-15 minutes, stirring occasionally, until the chicken is almost cooked and the gravy thickens. Adjust salt to taste.
  • step10:In a heavy-bottomed pot or a large oven-safe dish, layer half of the partially cooked rice at the bottom.
  • step11: Spread the chicken and gravy mixture evenly over the rice layer.
  • step12: Cover with the remaining rice.
  • step13: Drizzle the saffron milk (if using) and the plain milk over the rice.
  • step14: Top with the reserved fried onions, chopped cilantro, and mint leaves.
  • step15:If using biryani masala, sprinkle it over the top.
  • step16:Cover the pot with a tight-fitting lid. If the lid is not tight enough, seal the edges with dough to trap the steam.
  • step17:Place the pot on a tava (griddle) over low heat and cook for 20-25 minutes. Alternatively, you can place the pot in a preheated oven at 350°F (175°C) for 25-30 minutes.
  • step18: Once done, remove from heat and let it rest for 10 minutes before opening the lid.
  • step19:Gently fluff the biryani with a fork to mix the layers.
