Recipe Revel




Ingredients

  • Chickpeas (Garbanzo beans): 1 cup dried (or 2 cups canned)
  • Onions: 2 medium, finely chopped
  • Tomatoes: 3 medium, pureed or finely chopped
  • Garlic: 4-5 cloves, minced
  • Ginger: 1-inch piece, minced
  • Green chilies: 2, slit (optional)
  • Cumin seeds: 1 teaspoon
  • Turmeric powder: 1/2 teaspoon
  • Red chili powder: 1 teaspoon
  • Coriander powder: 1 teaspoon
  • Cumin powder: 1 teaspoon
  • Garam masala: 1 teaspoon
  • Chana masala powder: 2 teaspoons (optional but recommended)
  • Salt: to taste
  • Oil: 2 tablespoons
  • Fresh cilantro: for garnish
  • Lemon juice: 1 tablespoon

Process

  • step1: If using dried chickpeas, rinse and soak them in plenty of water overnight. Drain and rinse them the next day.
  • step2: Cook them in a pressure cooker with enough water and a pinch of salt until soft (about 3-4 whistles). If using canned chickpeas, drain and rinse them thoroughly.
  • step3: Puree the tomatoes in a blender or finely chop them.
  • step4: Heat oil in a large pan or pot over medium heat.
  • step5: Add the cumin seeds and let them splutter.
  • step6: Add the finely chopped onions and sauté until they turn golden brown.
  • step7: Add the minced garlic, ginger, and slit green chilies. Sauté for another 2-3 minutes until fragrant.
  • step8: Add the turmeric powder, red chili powder, coriander powder, and cumin powder. Sauté for a minute to let the spices bloom.
  • step9: Add the pureed or chopped tomatoes. Cook until the tomatoes break down and the oil starts to separate from the mixture, about 10 minutes.
  • step10: Add the cooked chickpeas to the pan. Mix well.
  • step11: Add salt to taste, garam masala, and chana masala powder (if using). Stir to combine.
  • step12: Add about 1 cup of water to the pan. Bring to a boil, then reduce the heat and let it simmer for 15-20 minutes.
  • step13: Adjust the consistency by adding more water if needed. The gravy should be thick and coat the chickpeas well.
  • step14: Check for seasoning and adjust salt and spices as needed.
  • step15: Stir in the lemon juice for a hint of tanginess.
  • step16: Garnish with fresh cilantro.
