Recipe Revel




Ingredients

  • Paneer: 250 grams, cubed
  • Butter: 2 tablespoons
  • Onions: 2 medium, finely chopped
  • Tomatoes: 3 large, pureed
  • Garlic: 4-5 cloves, minced
  • Ginger: 1-inch piece, minced
  • Green chilies: 1-2, slit (optional)
  • Cashew nuts: 10-12, soaked in warm water for 15 minutes
  • Cream: 1/4 cup
  • Kasuri methi (dried fenugreek leaves): 1 teaspoon, crushed
  • Garam masala: 1 teaspoon
  • Turmeric powder: 1/2 teaspoon
  • Red chili powder: 1 teaspoon
  • Coriander powder: 1 teaspoon
  • Cumin seeds: 1 teaspoon
  • Salt: to taste
  • Sugar: 1 teaspoon (optional)
  • Cilantro (coriander leaves): for garnish

Process

  • step1: Puree the tomatoes in a blender until smooth. Set aside.
  • step2: Soak the cashew nuts in warm water for 15 minutes, then blend into a smooth paste with a little water.
  • step3: Cube the paneer and optionally, lightly fry it in a bit of oil until golden. This helps to firm up the paneer and enhance its flavor.
  • step4: Heat the butter and oil in a pan over medium heat. The oil prevents the butter from burning.
  • step5: Add the cumin seeds and let them splutter.
  • step6: Add the finely chopped onions and sauté until golden brown.
  • step7: Add the minced garlic, ginger, and green chilies. Sauté for another 2-3 minutes until fragrant.
  • step8: Add the turmeric powder, red chili powder, and coriander powder. Sauté for a minute to let the spices bloom.
  • step9: Pour in the tomato puree and cook until the oil starts to separate from the sauce, about 10-15 minutes.
  • step10: Stir in the cashew paste, mix well, and cook for another 5 minutes. This will thicken the gravy and add richness.
  • step11: Add salt and sugar (if using), and adjust to taste.
  • step12: Add the paneer cubes and gently mix to coat them with the sauce.
  • step13: Stir in the cream and crushed kasuri methi. Let it simmer for a few minutes until the paneer is heated through.
  • step14: Finish with a sprinkle of garam masala and garnish with fresh cilantro.
