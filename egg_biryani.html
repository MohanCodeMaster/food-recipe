Recipe Revel




Ingredients

  • Basmati rice: 2 cups
  • Water: 4 cups
  • Bay leaf: 1
  • Cinnamon stick: 1-inch piece
  • Cloves: 3
  • Cardamom pods: 2
  • Salt: 1 teaspoon
  • Eggs: 6, hard-boiled and peeled
  • Turmeric powder: 1/2 teaspoon
  • Red chili powder: 1/2 teaspoon+
  • Salt: a pinch
  • Oil: 2 tablespoons
  • Onions: 3 large, thinly sliced
  • Tomatoes: 2 medium, chopped
  • Green chilies: 2, slit
  • Ginger-garlic paste: 2 tablespoons
  • Yogurt: 1/2 cup
  • Mint leaves: 1/4 cup, chopped
  • Cilantro: 1/4 cup, chopped
  • Lemon juice: 2 tablespoons
  • Oil: 3 tablespoons
  • Ghee: 2 tablespoons
  • Turmeric powder: 1/2 teaspoon
  • Red chili powder: 1 teaspoon
  • Coriander powder: 1 teaspoon
  • Cumin powder: 1/2 teaspoon
  • Garam masala: 1 teaspoon
  • Biryani masala: 1 tablespoon (optional)
  • Biryani masala: 1 tablespoon (optional)

Process

  • step1:Rinse the basmati rice in water until the water runs clear. Soak the rice in water for 30 minutes.
  • step2: In a large pot, bring 4 cups of water to a boil. Add the bay leaf, cinnamon stick, cloves, cardamom pods, and salt.
  • step3:Add the soaked rice and cook until the rice is 70-80% cooked (the rice should still have a bite to it). Drain the rice and set aside.
  • step4: Boil the eggs, peel them, and make small slits in the eggs to allow the spices to penetrate.
  • step5: Heat 2 tablespoons of oil in a pan. Add the turmeric powder, red chili powder, and a pinch of salt.
  • step6: Add the boiled eggs and sauté until they are lightly golden. Remove and set aside.
  • step7: In the same pan, add 3 tablespoons of oil and 2 tablespoons of ghee. Heat over medium heat.
  • step8: Add the thinly sliced onions and fry until they turn golden brown and caramelized.
  • step9: This may take about 10-15 minutes. Remove half of the fried onions and set aside for layering and garnish.
  • step10:Add the ginger-garlic paste and slit green chilies to the remaining onions in the pan.
  • step11: Sauté for 2-3 minutes until the raw smell disappears.
  • step12:Add the chopped tomatoes and cook until they become soft and the oil starts to separate.
  • step13: Add the turmeric powder, red chili powder, coriander powder, cumin powder, garam masala, and biryani masala (if using). Cook for another 2-3 minutes.
  • step14:Add the yogurt, chopped mint, and cilantro. Mix well and cook for 2-3 minutes.
  • step15:Add the sautéed eggs to the mixture and gently coat them with the masala. Cook for 5 minutes, allowing the eggs to absorb the flavors
  • step16:In a heavy-bottomed pot, layer half of the partially cooked rice at the bottom.
  • step17: Spread the egg masala mixture evenly over the rice layer.
  • step18: Cover with the remaining rice.
  • step19:Drizzle the lemon juice over the rice.
  • step20:Top with the reserved fried onions and a few mint and cilantro leaves.
  • step21: Cover the pot with a tight-fitting lid. If the lid is not tight enough, seal the edges with dough to trap the steam.
  • step22:Place the pot on a tava (griddle) over low heat and cook for 20-25 minutes. Alternatively, you can place the pot in a preheated oven at 350°F (175°C) for 25-30 minutes.
  • step23: Once done, remove from heat and let it rest for 10 minutes before opening the lid.
  • step24: Gently fluff the biryani with a fork to mix the layers.
