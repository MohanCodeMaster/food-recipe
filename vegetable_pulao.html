Recipe Revel




Ingredients

  • Basmati rice: 1 cup
  • Mixed vegetables: 1 1/2 cups (carrots, peas, green beans, potatoes, bell peppers), chopped into bite-sized pieces
  • Onion: 1 medium, thinly sliced
  • Tomatoes: 1 medium, chopped
  • Green chilies: 2, slit (optional)
  • Garlic: 4 cloves, minced
  • Ginger: 1-inch piece, minced
  • Cilantro: for garnish
  • Mint leaves: a handful, chopped (optional)
  • Ghee or oil: 2 tablespoons
  • Water: 2 cups
  • Cumin seeds: 1 teaspoon
  • Bay leaf: 1
  • Cloves: 4
  • Cardamom pods: 3
  • Cinnamon stick: 1-inch piece
  • Star anise: 1 (optional)
  • Peppercorns: 5-6
  • Turmeric powder: 1/2 teaspoon
  • Red chili powder: 1 teaspoon
  • Garam masala: 1 teaspoon

Process

  • step1: Rinse the basmati rice under running water until the water runs clear.
  • step2: Soak the rice in water for 20-30 minutes. Drain and set aside.
  • step3: Chop the mixed vegetables into bite-sized pieces.
  • step4: Thinly slice the onion and chop the tomatoes.
  • step5: Mince the garlic and ginger.
  • step6: Heat ghee or oil in a large pot or pressure cooker over medium heat.
  • step7: Add the whole spices: cumin seeds, bay leaf, cloves, cardamom pods, cinnamon stick, star anise (if using), and peppercorns. Sauté for a few seconds until fragrant.
  • step8: Add the sliced onions and sauté until they turn golden brown.
  • step9: Add the minced garlic, ginger, and slit green chilies. Sauté for another 2-3 minutes until fragrant.
  • step10: Add the chopped tomatoes and cook until they soften and the oil starts to separate from the mixture.
  • step11: Add the mixed vegetables and stir well.
  • step12: Add the ground spices: turmeric powder, red chili powder, and garam masala. Mix well to coat the vegetables with the spices.
  • step13: Add the drained rice and gently mix to combine with the vegetables and spices.
  • step14: Pour in 2 cups of water and add salt to taste. Stir well.
  • step15: Bring the mixture to a boil. If using a pressure cooker, cover with the lid and cook for 1 whistle on medium heat, then turn off the heat and let the pressure release naturally.
  • step16: If using a pot, cover with a tight-fitting lid, reduce the heat to low, and cook for 15-20 minutes until the rice is cooked and the water is absorbed.
  • step17: Once the rice is cooked, fluff it gently with a fork.
  • step18: Garnish with chopped cilantro and mint leaves (if using).
