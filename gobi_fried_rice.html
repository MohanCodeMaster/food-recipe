Recipe Revel




Ingredients

  • Cooked rice: 2 cups (preferably cooled, leftover rice)
  • Cauliflower (gobi): 1 small, cut into small florets
  • Onion: 1 medium, finely chopped
  • Capsicum (bell pepper): 1 small, finely chopped
  • Carrots: 1 small, finely chopped
  • Green peas: 1/2 cup (fresh or frozen)
  • Green chilies: 2, slit (optional)
  • Garlic: 4 cloves, minced
  • Ginger: 1-inch piece, minced
  • Soy sauce: 2 tablespoons
  • Vinegar: 1 teaspoon
  • Pepper powder: 1/2 teaspoon
  • Salt: to taste
  • Oil: 3 tablespoons
  • Spring onions: for garnish, chopped

Process

  • step1: Wash and cut the cauliflower into small florets.
  • step2: Finely chop the onion, bell pepper, and carrots.
  • step3: Mince the garlic and ginger.
  • step4: If using fresh green peas, blanch them in boiling water for 2-3 minutes and then drain. If using frozen peas, thaw them.
  • step5: Heat 1 tablespoon of oil in a large pan or wok over medium heat.
  • step6: Add the cauliflower florets and stir-fry until they start to turn golden brown and are cooked through but still crisp.
  • step7: This should take about 5-7 minutes. Remove the cauliflower from the pan and set aside.
  • step8: In the same pan, add the remaining 2 tablespoons of oil.
  • step9: Add the minced garlic, ginger, and slit green chilies (if using). Sauté for 1-2 minutes until fragrant.
  • step10: Add the finely chopped onions and sauté until they turn translucent.
  • step11: Add the chopped carrots and stir-fry for 2-3 minutes.
  • step12: Add the chopped bell pepper and green peas. Stir-fry for another 2-3 minutes until the vegetables are cooked but still crisp.
  • step13: Add the stir-fried cauliflower back to the pan with the vegetables.
  • step14: Add the cooked rice to the pan. Gently mix to combine all the ingredients.
  • step15: Pour in the soy sauce and vinegar. Stir well to evenly coat the rice and vegetables.
  • step16: Add salt and pepper powder to taste. Mix well.
  • step17: Stir-fry for another 2-3 minutes, allowing the flavors to meld together.
  • step18: Garnish with chopped spring onions.
